Feature Details

Purpose

Water-level monitoring and feature extraction of the Narmada River Front in Jabalpur City using high-accuracy drone-based geospatial data.

Key Deliverables

  • High-resolution orthomosaic imagery
  • DEM, DSM, and DTM generation
  • Dense point cloud & 3D terrain models

Project Objective

To conduct a high-accuracy drone-based photogrammetry survey of the Narmada River Front, Jabalpur to generate engineering-grade geospatial datasets supporting riverfront planning, irrigation management, water-level monitoring, and sustainable urban development.

  • UAV-based high-resolution aerial data acquisition
  • Accurate spatial baseline using GCPs & SOI benchmarks
  • Reliable data for planning, monitoring, and development

Key Objectives & Intended Use

  • DGCA-compliant UAV survey of both river banks
  • Feature-level digitization of:
    • River edges & ghats
    • Roads, built-up areas & terrain features
  • GIS & CAD compatible datasets for DPRs and tenders
  • Support for:
    • Riverfront development
    • Irrigation & water resource management
    • Flood & water-level assessment
